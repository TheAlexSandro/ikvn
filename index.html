Intro



My name is Kevin Yustinus, I started using Telegram in 2019. At that time I used Telegram just for fun, not for making big projects..

I started making projects in telegram in 2020, at that time I also started learning to make telegram bots using JS (JavaScript). I learned to make telegram bots for about 2 months, at that time I thought about stopping making bots because I think making bots is a difficult thing. But in 2021, I started to bounce back to learn to make telegram bots, finally... after learning many times I was able to make a bot, the first bot I made was Help Bot. After the bot was created, I thought of publishing it... But after I published it, no one visited the bot. Finally, in September 2021, I made the bot private (meaning only I can use it).
